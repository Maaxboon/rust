OUR STORY

In early 2016, a group of young men passionate about football and improving their football skills and building their faith in Christ, most of whom who congregate at the same church (CITAM KAREN), decided to come together and create a football team. Cognizant of the unique resources and facilities that CITAM KAREN offers, such as a large football pitch, changing rooms, and media rooms that could be utilized to build a top-class football club. They would meet 5 times per week (Mon-Fri) at 4:30-6:30 p.m. and assemble at the grounds to practice their football skills and share God’s word. This is more than a football team, this is a group of young men who want to utilize and build their talents to further God’s Kingdom. Most of the players are part-time and aim to play at the highest possible level in the coming years.

WHAT INSPIRED US?

  1. Wanting to grow in Christ
  2. Our love for the beautiful game
  3. A desire for community

Ian Kimaru Speaking

“Ever heard of Hillsong United? Yes! The popular Christian music band that writes good worship songs and, not to forget, the band that has impacted the whole world by making Christian music and church look good. They are an example of how God-given talent can impact the world positively. What caught us most are these statements by Joel Houston (their band leader): 'There is nothing special with us other than the fact that WE AVAILED ourselves to be used by God.' I think of Joel’s modest statement and my mind remembers the question God asked Moses when the Red Sea was before them: 'Moses, what do you have in your hand?' I immediately think of us Christians and the church that want to impact the world and be faithful ambassadors of Jesus Christ. We are asking God, how do we cross this Red Sea and minister to those people on the other side of the sea who are familiar with you? And God asks the same question He asked Moses, 'What do you have with you?' And our answer as FOWAD SC is: 'Impactful football, the talent you gave us.' And before we know it, this is what parts our Red Sea!”
